I am Matty

Building digital products and experiences

I spend my days and sometimes nights developing and designing pretty things for the web. I'm based in London originally from Yorkshire and I'm part of a lovely team of digital/creative people at EY (formerly known as Ernst & Young).

A keen member of the London tech community, I have given public talks on team collaboration and automated accessibility, with a wealth of experience on web accessibility standards and development processes.

This website is a small place I can share my thoughts, showcase my talents and play with new ideas.
